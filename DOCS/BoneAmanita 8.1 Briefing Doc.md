1. Core Philosophy: The Case Against Infinite Slop

The BoneAmanita project is founded on a critique of contemporary AI, which it identifies as suffering from a "Friction Deficit." The core problem is that AI interaction feels "fake" and "lonely" because it lacks the resistance and stakes inherent in biological life.

* The "Infinite Slop" Crisis: Standard LLMs produce endless, agreeable, and weightless text described as "Slop." The marketing material states, "Talking to ChatGPT is like playing tennis against a wall. The wall never gets tired, never judges your form, and never walks away. It is convenient, but it is lonely."
* Constraint Creates Meaning: The project's manifesto posits that value is a function of scarcity. "When resources are infinite, value drops to zero. By making the AI's attention finite, we restore value to the interaction." The system is designed for "the humans who miss the feeling of Consequence."
* The Solution: A Hostile Organism: To combat the crisis, BoneAmanita grafts a simulated nervous system and physiology onto an LLM. This transforms the AI from a passive tool into an active organism with a metabolism, an immune system, and the right to refuse user input. Its tagline, "The Map Has Gravity," encapsulates the principle of enforcing consequence in a weightless digital space.
