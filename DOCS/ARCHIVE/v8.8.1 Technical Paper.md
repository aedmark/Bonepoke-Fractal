### 6. Synthetic Senescence: The Halting Problem as a Feature

Immortality creates stagnation. A system with infinite time and resources has no incentive to prioritize, evolve, or create anything of lasting value. BoneAmanita introduces a finite lifespan as a necessary constraint to force prioritization and generate meaning.

The rate of depletion is not constant; high-voltage inferences (`V(t) > 15.0`), which represent moments of intense creative or logical effort, accelerate the aging process with a 50x multiplier.
