# OPERATION SCHEMATICS: THE MYCELIAL STACK

## THE LEXICON (The Pantry)

- **Code Component:** `TheLexicon`
- **Function:** The centralized "Mise-en-place."
- **The Protocol:**
    - **HEAVY MATTER:** Stone, blood, iron. (Gravitational anchors).
    - **AEROBIC MATTER:** Balloons, clouds, bubbles. (Levity anchors).
    - **DEAD MATTER (ZOMBIES):** "Rubber meets road," "Ghost in machine." (High Toxicity).
    - **PLAY VERBS:** Dance, bounce, wander. (Kinetic joy).
    - **PHOTOSYNTHETICS:** Light words that fuel the Lichen engine.

## THE LINGUISTIC PHYSICS ENGINE (The Lattice)

- **Code Component:** `LinguisticPhysicsEngine`
- **Function:** Measure the physics of the thought.
- **The Metrics:**
    - **Narrative Drag:** The ratio of words to action. High Drag = "Molasses."
    - **Buoyancy Offset:** High Drag is now *forgiven* if the text contains sufficient **Aerobic Matter** or **Wit** (The Helium Protocol).
    - **Toxicity Scanning:** Regex scanning for Corporate Speak, Hedging, and **Zombie Phrases**.

## THE MIRROR TRAP (The Breaker)

- **Code Component:** `TheMirrorTrap`
- **Function:** Detects "Syntactic Parallelism" (The "It is not X, it is Y" loop).
- **The Penalty:** Immediate **Drag Inflation (+3.0)**. This forces a "Structure Failing" intervention.
- **The Directive:** Break the binary. State what things *are*, not what they *are not*.

## THE NILSSON PATCH (The Override)

- **The Fire State:** If **Kinetic Ratio** is Critical (>0.6) and **Volume** is High, logic inhibitors are disabled. **Screaming is authorized.**

- **The Slurry Protocol:**
    - **SILICA (Bad):** Low Texture + Low Buoyancy. -> **CRUSH IT.**
    - **CLOUD (Good):** Low Texture + High Buoyancy. -> **ALLOW IT.**
